About us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering has been in existence since the inception of the Institution. Given the high demand for Computer Science professionals from industry, the department offers undergraduate programs,post-graduate degree programme and Doctoral Degree Program in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. All the programs offered are approved by the National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi.
